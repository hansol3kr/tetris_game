Privacy Policy — Blockfall: Neon Drop

Last updated: July 15, 2026

Short version: Blockfall does not collect, store, or share any personal information. There are no accounts, no ads, and no analytics or tracking. Your game data stays on your device.

Who we are

Blockfall: Neon Drop (“the App”) is an independent game developed by an individual developer (“we”). This policy explains how the App handles information.

Information we collect

None. The App does not collect personal data. There is no sign-up, login, or account. We do not use advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs of any kind.

Data stored on your device

Your game progress, settings, statistics, and saved replays are stored locally on your device only. This data never leaves your device and is not sent to us or to any third party. Deleting the App removes this data.

Online multiplayer (optional)

If you choose to play an online 1‑versus‑1 match, the App connects you with another player. To establish that connection, standard network information (such as your device’s IP address) is processed transiently by a matchmaking/relay service only for the duration of the match, solely to route gameplay between players in real time. It is not stored, is not used to identify you, and is not shared for any other purpose. Single‑player and offline modes involve no network activity.

Advertising

The App currently does not display advertisements. If a future update introduces ads, this policy and the App Store privacy disclosures will be updated before that feature is released.

Children’s privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children.

Third‑party platforms

The App is distributed through the Apple App Store and Google Play. Those platforms have their own privacy policies governing your account and your use of their services.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
